Bunn is an ordinary bunny who lives in the village.
He comes from a family line of farmers.
His family's main income comes from farming.
But, two months ago, he started living alone
because his grandfather, his one and only family passed away.
He only has one caring neighbour who is a merchant.
The neighbour's name is Hare and he is as old as Bunn's grandpa.
Bunn lived through his day with sorrow after his grandpa died.
However, recently, a thief sneaked into Bunn's house.
The thief stole all of Bunn's money, 
leaving only a weird letter beside the table. (Go to table and press C)
